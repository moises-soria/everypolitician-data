Peter Skaarup, born May 1st 1964 in Århus, son of retired photo technician Hans Skaarup and former Member of Parliament, accountant Birthe Skaarup.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Danish People's Party in Copenhagen greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Member of Parliament for the Danish People's Party in Eastern Copenhagen constituency from March 11th 1998 to November 13th 2007.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Utterslev nomination district from 2007 to 2011.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Brønshøj nomination district from 1997.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Husum nomination district from 1997 to 2006.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Østbane nomination district from 1997 to 2006.

Parliamentary career

Chairman of the Legal Affairs Committee from 2015 and 2005-2011. Member of the Legal Affairs Committee, The Immigration, Integration and Housing Committee, the Cultural Affairs Committee, the Standing Orders Committee, the Foreign Policy Committee and NATO's Parliamentary Assembly (NPA). Member of the EU Convention 2002-2004. Chairman of the parliamentary group of the Danish People's Party from 2012, vice-chairman 1998-2012. Vice-chairman of the Danish People's Party 1998-2012.


Education and jobs
Aarhus Cathedral Upper Secondary School, from 1979 to 1982.
Forældre School in Aarhus, from 1970 to 1979.

Head of secretariat for the Danish People's Party, the Folketing, from 1995 to 1998.
Head of secretariat and party secretary for the Progress Party, the Folketing, from 1990 to 1995.
Technical consultant, DSB (the Danish state-owned railway company), travel agency division, from 1986 to 1990.


Affiliations
Member of the council of representatives of Krogerup High School 1988-2002. Member of the Danish Parliaments's Data Processing Steering Committee 1992-1997. Member of Østerbro Local Council 1995-1997. Member of the council of representatives of the Institute for Danish Foreign Policy (DUPI) 1996-1999. Member of Copenhagen City Council 1998-2004, member of the Economic Affairs Committee. Member of the management committee of Copenhagen Capacity and of 'Bellevue Strandpark' 1998-2002. Member of the Fifth Committee of the Tax Appeal Board 1998-2002. Member of the Local Board of the Copenhagen Police District 1998-2002. Member of the board of directors of 'Ørestadsselskabet' and 'Østamagerbaneselskabet' (the East Amager Railway Company) 1998-2004. Member of the central board of the Danish People's Party from 1998. Member of the council of representatives of Atlantsammenslutningen from 2006. Deputy Member of the Advisory Council on Government Salaries from 2010. Member of the council of representatives of 'Danmarks Nationalbank' from 2016. Member of the National Tax Tribunal from 2016.


Publications
»Peter Skaarup - et biografisk portræt« (Peter Skaarup - a biographical portrait) by Niels Martinov, 2014


Awards
German Marshall Fund Fellow from 1999. Taiwan Friendship Medal of Diplomacy 2003. Knight of the Order of Dannebrog 2008, Knight of 1st Class of the Order of Dannebrog 2016.
