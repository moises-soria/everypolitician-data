Mogens Lykketoft

The Social Democratic Party,S

Member of the Danish Parliament
The Social Democratic Party

Former Speaker, former Minister
Trepilevej 1 B, 2930 Klampenborg
Phone in the Parliament: +45 3337 4034

Homepage: http://www.lykketoft.dk

Mogens Lykketoft, born January 9th 1946 in Copenhagen, son of paint dealer Axel Lykketoft and Martha Lykketoft. Married to journalist Mette Holm. Has two daugthers and five grandchildren.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Social Democratic Party in Greater Copenhagen greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Member of Parliament for the Social Democratic Party in Copenhagen County constituency from December 8th 1981 to November 13th 2007.
Candidate for the Social Democratic Party in Taastrup nomination district from 2007.
Candidate for the Social Democratic Party in Glostrup nomination district from 1989 to 2006.
Candidate for the Social Democratic Party in Amager nomination district from 1980 to 1989.

Parliamentary career
Minister for Foreign Affairs from December 21st 2000 to November 27th 2001.
Minister for Finance from January 25th 1993 to December 21st 2000.
Minister for Taxation from January 20th 1981 to September 10th 1982.
Speaker of the Danish Parliament from September 30th 2011 to June 18th 2015.
Member of the Presidium from September 8th 2009 to June 18th 2015.
Leave of Absence from the Danish Parliament to be President of the General Assembly of the United Nations July 4th - September 30th 2016.

Auditor of public accounts October 2006 - October 2011. Foreign policy spokesman 2005-2009, political spokesman 1991-1993 and 2001-2002, economic and political affairs spokesman and finance policy spokesman 1988-1991. Vice-chairman of the Foreign Policy Committee 2009-2011. Chairman of the Economic and Political Affairs Committee 1988-1990, the Trade and Industry Committee 1987-1988 and the Fiscal Affairs Committee 1984-1986. Chairman of the Social Democratic Party 2002-2005.

Education and jobs
M.Sc. (economics), University of Copenhagen, 1971.
Matriculated i mathematics, Frederiksberg Upper Secondary School, 1964.

Head of department, the Economic Council of the Labour Movement, from 1975 to 1981.
Employee, the Economic Council of the Labour Movement, from 1966 to 1981.
Over the years, secretary of the Danish Federation of Trade Unions' economic democracy and taxation committee, and the 'white paper committee' on housing policy.


Affiliations
Member of the management committee of the Social Democratic student association Frit Forum (Free Forum) 1965-1970, national chairman 1968-1969. Many posts within the party since 1965. Member of such bodies as the Income Tax Committee 1975-1977 and the Board of Assessors 1976-1981 and 1984-1993. President of the General Assembly of the United Nations 2015-2016.


Publications
Co-editor of working and principle programmes. Editor of »Magtspil og Sikkerhed« (Power Play and Security), 1968, and »Kravet om lighed« (The Demand for Equality), 1973. Co-author of such works as »Anno 2001 – en socialdemokratisk science fiction« (Anno 2001 – Social Democratic Science Fiction), 1986. Author of »Skattereform '78?« (Tax Reform '78?), 1978, »Sans og samling – en socialdemokratisk krønike« (Sense and Cohesion – A Social Democratic Chronicle), 1994, »Kina drager« (China allures) with Mette Holm, 2006, »Burma - Myanmar - fortællinger fra et land i forandring« (Burma - Myanmar - stories from a changing country) with Mette Holm, 2012 and »I verdens tjeneste – 15 måneder for FN« (In Global Service - 15 months in the United Nations) with Mette Holm, 2016.
