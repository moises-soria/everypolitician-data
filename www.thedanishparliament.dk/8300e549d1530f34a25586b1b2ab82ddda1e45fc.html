Peter Kofod Poulsen, born February 27th 1990, son of metal worker Karsten Poulsen and nurse Birte Kofod Poulsen.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Danish People's Party in South Jutland greater constituency from June 18th 2015.
Candidate for the Danish People's Party in Aabenraa nomination district from 2013.

Parliamentary career

Spokesman on legal affairs and social dumping. Member of the Employment Committee, the European Affairs Committee, the Legal Affairs Committee, the Transport and Building Committee, the Foreign Affairs Committee ant the Immigration, Integration and Housing Committee.


Education and jobs
Schoolteacher, UC Syddanmark, Haderslev, from 2011 to 2015.
Basic military training, Danish Army, Telegraph Regiment, Fredericia, 2010.
Upper Secondary School certificate, Bornholms Gymnasium, 2006-2009., from 2006 to 2009.


Affiliations
Member of the Town Council in Haderslev since 2014, Chairman of the Social Committee, 2014-2015. Member of the Regional Council in the Region of Southern Denmark, 2014-2015. National Chairman of Dansk Folkepartis Ungdom (Danish People's Party – Youth section), 2015.

Last updated: [03.10.2016]
