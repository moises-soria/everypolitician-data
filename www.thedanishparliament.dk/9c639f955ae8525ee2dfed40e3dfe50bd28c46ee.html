Bjarne Laustsen, born December 9th 1953 in Skivum, son of Harry Laustsen and Anna Laustsen.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Social Democratic Party in North Jutland greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Member of Parliament for the Social Democratic Party in North Jutland County constituency from March 11th 1998 to November 13th 2007.
Member of Parliament for the Social Democratic Party in North Jutland County constituency from November 11th 1992 to September 20th 1994.
Candidate for the Social Democratic Party in Frederikshavn nomination district from 1997.
Candidate for the Social Democratic Party in Aars nomination district from 1988 to 1991.

Parliamentary career

Vice-chairman of The Employment Committee. Member of the Energy-, Supply and Climate Committee, the Environment and Food Committee, the Defence Committee and The Scrutineers’ Committee. Chairman of the Labour Market Committee 2000- 2001.


Education and jobs
Trained as a machine fitter at the 'Norden' company.
Østhimmerland Youth School, 1969.
Skivum School, from 1960 to 1967.

Jørlunde School of Metalworking, from 1994 to 1998.
Employed at Hydrema, Støvring, from 1976 to 1992.


Affiliations
Chairman of 'Metal Ungdom' (the Young Metalworkers Union), Aalborg. Member of the school board of Karens Minde School 1978-1985. Shop steward at Hydrema in Støvring 1978-1992. Member of the management committee of the Social Democratic Party in Støvring from 1982, chairman 1984-1986. Member of the management committee of the Danish Metalworkers Union in Aalborg from 1985. Chairman of the Social Democrats in the Metalworkers Union in Aalborg 1985-2001. Branch chairman in Aars nomination district 1986-1988. Member of the Town Council of Støvring Municipality 1986-1993. Visiting teacher, School Contact, Aalborg 1987-1992. President of the Association of Emergency Management Agencies 2006-2014 and from 2015. Civil Commissioner of the Danish Home Guard from 2013.
