Johan Henrik Dahl, born February 20th 1960 in Vejlby-Risskov, son of vicar Paul Dahl and university lecturer Inger Marie Dahl. Partner Christina Yoon Petersen.


Member period
Member of Parliament for Liberal Alliance in South Jutland greater constituency from June 18th 2015.
Candidate for Liberal Alliance in all nomination districts in the constituency from 2014.

Parliamentary career

Spokesman on foreign affairs, education from 2016, higher education and research, on rural districts and islands and anti-bureaucracy spokesman since 2015. Vice-chairman of the Research, Innovation and Further Education Committee from 2015.


Education and jobs
MA, Copenhagen Business School, from 1990 to 1993.
MA in Communications , University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, USA, 1985-1986 and 1987-1988..
M.Sc. Sociology, University of Copenhagen, from 1980 to 1987.
Language officer (Russian), Copenhagen, from 1978 to 1980.

Author and speaker, Copenhagen, from 2009 to 2015.
Critic, Weekendavisen (newspaper), from 2009 to 2014.
Head of Development, Niras A/S (consultancy group), Allerød, from 2008 to 2009.
Radio presenter, DR P1, Copenhagen, from 2004 to 2005.
Assigned professor, Copenhagen Business School, from 2003 to 2008.
Co-owner, Explora A/S, Copenhagen, from 1999 to 2008.
Head of Research, AC Nielsen AIM, Copenhagen, from 1994 to 1998.
Lecturer, Roskilde University, from 1993 to 1994.
Lecturer (temp) , Copenhagen Business School, from 1990 to 1993.
Media researcher, Danmarks Radio (broadcasting company), from 1988 to 1990.


Affiliations
Deputy Chairman of Sammenslutningen af Medieforskere i Danmark (association of media researchers in Denmark), 1993-1994. Member of the BioTIK-group in the Danish Ministry of Business, 1997-2000. Member of Nyt Europa's (a non-profit-making organisation which works for a progressive European Community) working committee, 1998-2001. Member of Anti Doping Danmark's information committee, 2000-2003. Member of Værdikommissionen (parliamentary commission to investigate values in Denmark), 2011-2012.


Publications
Author of »Hvis din nabo var en bil« (If your neighbour were a car), Akademisk Forlag, 1997, »Den kronologiske uskyld« (Chronological innocence), Gyldendal, 1998, »Mindernes land« (The land of memories), Gyldendal, 2005, »Den usynlige verden« (The invisible world), Gyldendal, 2008, »Spildte kræfter« (Waste of energy), Gyldendal, 2011 and a novel »NT«, People’s Press, 2013. Co-author of »Marketing og semiotik« (Marketing and semiotics), Akademisk Forlag, 1993, »Borgerlige ord efter revolutionen« (Post-revolution bourgeois words), Gyldendal, 1999, »Det ny systemskifte« (The new system change), Gyldendal, 2001, »Epostler« (E-postles) Gyldendal, 2003 and »Krigeren, borgeren og taberen« (The warrior, the citizen and the loser), Gyldendal, 2006. Also the author of a large number of contributions to anthologies and newspapers since 1995.


Awards
Rosenkjærprisen (award) 1998. Columbus-prisen (award) 2000.
