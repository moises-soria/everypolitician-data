Karen Johanne Klint, born October 6th 1947 in Hemmerslev, daughter of smallholder Chr. Regnar Jensen and Inger Klint Jensen.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Social Democratic Party in South Jutland greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Member of Parliament for the Social Democratic Party in Vejle County constituency from March 11th 1998 to November 13th 2007.
Candidate for the Social Democratic Party in Vejle South nomination district from 2007.
Candidate for the Social Democratic Party in Vejle nomination district from 1998 to 2006.

Parliamentary career

Spokeswoman on ecclesiastical affairs and the Danish Council of Ethics. Chairwoman of the Ecclesiastical Affairs Committee and the Committee on the Danish Council of Ethics. Vice-chairwoman of the Naturalization Committee, the Scrutineers’ Committee and the Supervisory Board in accordance with Section 71 of the Constitutional Act. Member of the Social Affairs Committee, the Standing Orders Committee, the Immigration and Integration Affairs Committee and the Nordic Council. Clerk of Parliament from 2007.


Education and jobs
One-year further training course, the Social Educator High School, Gedved, 1994.
Holder of a business diploma in personnel management, Vejle Business College.
Social educator, 1970.
Lower secondary school leaving examination.

Acting head of the Socio-Psychiatric Centre, Sønderparken, Vejle County, from 1994 to 1998.
Deputy in the Adult and Handicap Department, Vejle County, from 1988 to 1993.
Educational consultant, Vejle County Social Services Department, from 1980 to 1987.
Care assistant and head of department, Brejning Reception Centre, from 1970 to 1980.
Trainee, Brejning Reception Centre, from 1967 to 1970.


Affiliations
Current affiliations: Member of Bredballe parish council. Member of the governing body of Ringgården, alcohol treatment home run by the YMCA's Social Work, Middelfart. Member of the governing body of Bøgevang School, a domestic science school for young people with special needs, Vejle. Member of the election committee in the Vejle South nomination district for the Social Democratic Party. Member of the board of representatives of EGV. Member of the board of Møltrup Optagelseshjem, Vildbjerg. Former affiliations: Member of the executive committee of 'Åndsvageforsorgens Personaleforbund' (The Mentally Handicapped Care Staff Association) 1976-1979. Member of Børkop Town Council 1978-1980 and of Vejle Town Council 1986-1998. Member of the Regional Association of Local Authorities in Vejle County 1990-1994. Member of the management committee of Jelling Teacher Training College 1990-1998. Member of the executive committee of the Danish Association of School Libraries 1990-1998. Chairwoman of 'Social Udviklings Fond' (the Social Development Foundation) (SUF) 1995-1998. Member of the management committee of 'Vejlefjord Centre for Brain Damage' 1999-2013. Member of the governing body of several institutions and party organisations.
