Pernille Skipper, born July 10th 1984 in Aalborg, daughter of former production manager Jørnn Skipper and consultant Henriette von Platen.


Member period
Member of Parliament for The Red-Green Alliance in Funen greater constituency from September 15th 2011.
Candidate for The Red-Green Alliance in Odense East nomination district from 2009.

Parliamentary career

Spokeswoman on political affairs, social services and gender equality. Vice-chairwoman of the parliamentary group of the Red-Green Alliance from 2011.


Education and jobs
Master of Law , University of Copenhagen, from 2004 to 2011.
Matriculation certificate, Aalborghus Upper Secondary School, from 2000 to 2003.
Tenth form primary school leaving examination, Østvendsyssels Youth School, 2000.

Student helper, Hillerød Court, from 2009 to 2011.
Student helper, the Centre for Legal and Police Research, from 2005 to 2006.


Affiliations
Member of the Red-Green Alliance from 2001. Chairwoman of the student's council at Aalborghus Upper Secondary School and DGS North Jutland 2001-2003. Member of the management committee of the Union of Danish Upper Secondary School Students 2001-2004. Vice-chairwoman of the Students' Council at the University of Copenhagen 2006-2007. Vice-chairwoman of the University of Copenhagen Education Strategy Unit 2006-2008. Member of the central board and Executive Committee of the Red-Green Alliance from 2009.

Last updated: [31.03.2017]
