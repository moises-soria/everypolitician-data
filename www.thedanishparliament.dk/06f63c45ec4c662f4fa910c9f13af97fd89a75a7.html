Hans Nicolai Andersen, born November 1st 1974 in Hillerød, son of senior clerk Uffe Andersen and clinical dietician Inge Lise Andersen.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in North Zealand greater constituency from September 15th 2011.
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in Frederiksborg County constituency from November 20th 2001 to November 13th 2007.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Frederikssund nomination district from 2012.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Helsingør nomination district from 1999 to 2012.

Parliamentary career

Spokesman on labour market from 2013, spokesman on disabled persons 2011-2013. Member of the Employment Committee, the Cultural Affairs Committee, the Gender Equality Committee, the Social Affairs Committee, the Health Committee, the Transport and Building Committee, the Research, Innovation and Further Education Committee and the Supervisory Board in accordance with Section 71 of the Constitutional Act.


Education and jobs
MSc (business administration and auditing), Copenhagen Business School, from 1997 to 2000.
BSc (business economics), Copenhagen Business School, from 1994 to 1997.
Higher commercial examination, Frederikssund Business School, from 1991 to 1994.
Kingo School, 6th-10th form, Slangerup, from 1986 to 1991.
Slangerup School, 1st - 5th form, Slangerup, from 1981 to 1986.

Business Controller, Berendsen Textil Service, from 2008 to 2011.
Business Controller, 'Falck Danmark', from 2000 to 2005.


Affiliations
Chairman of the Young Liberals at Slangerup 1992-1999. Member of Slangerup Town Council 1994-2000, first Deputy Mayor 1998-2000. Member of Frederiksborg County Council from 1998, chairman of the Social Services Committee 2002-2006. Chairman of Frederiksborg Linnedservice A/S 1998-2001. Chairman of the Preventive Council in Frederiksborg County 2002-2006. Chairman of the social policy committee of the Liberal Party's national organisation 2004-2006. Member of Helsingør Town Council 2006-2013, chairman of the Occupational and Labour Market Committe, member of the Children and Young People Committee, chairman of the Liberal Party's Town Council group. Member of the Regional Council of the Capital Region 2010-2011. Member of Frederikssund Town Council from 2014.
