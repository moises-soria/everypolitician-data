Eva Kjer Hansen, born August 22th 1964 in Aabenraa, daughter of farmer Hans Hansen and Ellen Kjer Hansen. Has three daughters.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in South Jutland greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in Vejle County constituency from December 12th 1990 to November 13th 2007.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Kolding South nomination district from 2007.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Kolding nomination district from 1990 to 2007.

Parliamentary career
Minister for Environment and Food from June 28th 2015 to February 29th 2016.
Minister for Food, Agriculture and Fisheries from September 12th 2007 to February 23rd 2010.
Minister for Social Affairs and Minister for Gender Equality from August 2nd 2004 to September 12th 2007.
Chairwoman of the European Affairs Committee October 2011 - June 2015.
Chairwoman of the Foreign Policy Committee March 2010 - September 2011.
Chairwoman of the Committee on Danish Cultural Affairs in South Slesvig 2002-2004.
Member of the European Parliament 1994-1999.

Education and jobs
MA (political science), University of Copenhagen, 2012.
Supplementary training at the Danish School of Journalism, from 1990 to 1991.
Aabenraa State School, from 1980 to 1983.
Rødekro School 1978-1980, from 1978 to 1980.
Hellevad School, from 1971 to 1978.


Awards
Commander of the Order of Dannebrog 2005.
