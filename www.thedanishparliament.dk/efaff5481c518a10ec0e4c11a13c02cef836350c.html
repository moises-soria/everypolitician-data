Nikolaj Barslund Villumsen, born February 28th 1983 in Aarhus.


Member period
Member of Parliament for The Red-Green Alliance in East Jutland greater constituency from June 18th 2015.
Member of Parliament for The Red-Green Alliance in Zealand greater constituency from September 15th 2011 to June 18th 2015.
Candidate for The Red-Green Alliance in Århus East nomination district from 2012.
Candidate for The Red-Green Alliance in Holbæk nomination district 2011.

Parliamentary career

Spokesman on foreign affairs and  emergency preparedness. Member of the Parliamentary Assembly of the European Council from 2011, vice-chairman of the group Unified European Left from 2013.


Education and jobs
Digital design and communication, the IT University, from 2009.
BA (history), University of Copenhagen, from 2005 to 2009.
Matriculated, Risskov County Upper Secondary School, from 2000 to 2003.
Primary school, Sølyst School, Egå, from 1989 to 1999.

International assistant, the Joint Council of Danish Students, from 2010 to 2011.
Collector, Museum of Copenhagen, from 2009 to 2010.
Intern with MEP Søren Søndergaard, the People's Movement against the EU, Brussels, 2008.
Scientific employee, the National Collection of Folklore, from 2007 to 2008.
Unskilled assistant, Møllehuset nursing home, 'De Gamles By', Nørrebro, from 2004 to 2009.
Helper for disabled people, from 2004 to 2008.
Volunteer, 'Operation Dagsværk' (work performed by young people for charity), from 2003 to 2004.


Affiliations
Member of the management board of 'Genstart Danmark' (Restart Denmark) 2007. Member of the management board of 'Udfordring Europa' (the European Challenge) 2007-2008. Member of the executive committee of the People's Movement against the EU 2008-2009. Member of the central board and executive committee of the Red-Green Alliance 2008-2011. Member of the coordination group and responsible for mobilisation at COP15 climate demonstration December 12th 2009. Member of the board of the election campaign for the Red-Green Alliance 2011. The Red-Green Alliance's representative in The Party of the European Left from 2013.

Last updated: [12.10.2015]
