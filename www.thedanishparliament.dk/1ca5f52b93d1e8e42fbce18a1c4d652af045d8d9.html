Maja Panduro Kyk, born May 19th 1982 in Randers, daughter of electrician Freddy Michael Mikkelsen Kjærulff and social and health care assistant Aase Panduro.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Social Democratic Party in East Jutland greater constituency from August 5th 2009.
Candidate for the Social Democratic Party in Randers South nomination district from 2007.

Parliamentary career

Spokeswoman on the environment from 2015, on political affairs 2014-2015 and on social affairs and on Nordic affairs 2011-2014.  Chairwoman of The Domestic and Social Affairs Committee. Member of the Environment and Food Committee. Former member of among others the Social Affairs Committee, the Foreign Affairs Committee, the Legal Affairs Committee, the Naturalisation Committee and the Immigration and Integration Affairs Committee. Vice-chairwoman of the Danish delegation to the Nordic Council from 2011-2012. Secretary of the parliamentary group of the Social Democratic Party from 2011-2013.


Education and jobs
Supplementary course in journalistic communication for BSc graduates, the Danish School of Media and Journalism, 2008.
BSc in political science, Aarhus University, 2007.
Basic Course in Adult Education, the Social Democratic Youth of Denmark, 2007.
Matriculated in the languages line, County Upper Secondary School, Paderup, 2002.

Freelance writer, 'Frivilligværket Randers' and the newspaper Information, from 2008 to 2009.
Laboratory assistant, DLG, Aarhus Harbour, 2004.
Service assistant at a grill bar in Randers, from 1999 to 2004.
Labourer, stores assistant, assembly, NT Randi, Randers, from 1997 to 2005.


Affiliations
Member of the management committee of the Danish Refugee Council's 'FrivilligHus' in Randers, vice-chairwoman 2002-2005. Member of Randers City Council for the Social Democratic Party 2006-2009, vice-chairwoman of the children's and education committee and member of the social affairs committee. Danish representative on the Nordic Youth Panel set up by the Norwegian Presidency of the Nordic Council of Ministers to pass on young people's recommendations regarding the future of the Nordic welfare model 2006. Coordinator for the Danish Refugee Council's national fund raising campaign in Randers and Purhus 2006. Ambassador for the Danish Cancer Society in Randers from 2007. Member of the management board of the Danish Cancer Society in Randers 2007-2008. Teacher with the Social Democratic Youth of Denmark 2007-2009. Teacher of young political activists in Lebanon 2009. Chairman of Ungdomsringen (youth organisation), 2012-2014. Member of the Board of the Danish National Bank, 2013-14. Member of the Board of The Economic Council of the Labour Movement (ECLM), 2014-2015. Relatives' Ambassador at the Danish Cancer Society's "Stafet for Livet" (relay run for life) since 2014.
