Finn Sørensen, born December 28th 1946 in Tagensbo Parish, son of factory worker Erik Sørensen and factory worker Astrid Sørensen.


Member period
Member of Parliament for The Red-Green Alliance in Copenhagen greater constituency from September 15th 2011.
Candidate for The Red-Green Alliance in Sundbyvester nomination district from 2015.
Candidate for The Red-Green Alliance in Utterslev nomination district from 2011.
Candidate for The Red-Green Alliance in Kolding North nomination district 2007.
Candidate for The Red-Green Alliance in Vejle South nomination district 2007.

Parliamentary career

Spokesman on municipal affairs from 2016, labour market and senior Citizens from 2015.


Education and jobs
No education.
Danish and English studies, University of Copenhagen, from 1967 to 1969.
Matriculation certificate in the modern languages line, 1965.

Vice-chairman, the United Federation of Danish Workers (Industry and Service), from 2005 to 2011.
Chairman of the Danish Brewery Workers' Union in Copenhagen, from 1986 to 2005.
Brewery worker, Carlsberg Depot and Tuborg, from 1978 to 1986.
Industrial worker, Nordisk Kabel og Tråd, LK Nes, Dansk Sojakagefabrik, from 1972 to 1978.
Actor, author and musician, with 'Røde Mor', among others, from 1968 to 1972.
Various unskilled jobs, from 1967 to 1972.


Affiliations
Member of the working party of 'Fagbevægelsen mod Unionen' (Trade Unions against the European Union), from 1991. Member of the board of 'Carl Scharnbergs Uofficielle fond' from 1997. Member of the United Federation of Danish Workers' Palestine Network from 2007.


Publications
Author of a large number of articles in left-wing media, including 'Dagbladet Arbejderen', 'Tidsskriftet Solidaritet' and 'Kritisk Debat'.

Last updated: [17.08.2016]
