Karen Ellemann Kloch, born August 26th 1969 in Charlottenlund, daughter of former Minister for Foreign Affairs Uffe Ellemann-Jensen and secretary Hanne Ellemann-Jensen. Married to Kresten Kloch. Mother of two children, born in 1998 and 1999.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Liberal Party in Greater Copenhagen greater constituency from November 13th 2007.
Candidate for the Liberal Party in Brøndby nomination district from 2007.

Parliamentary career
Minister for Equal Opportunities and Nordic Cooperation from November 28th 2016.
Minister for Social Affairs and the Interior from June 28th 2015 to November 28th 2016.
Minister for the Environment and Minister for Nordic Cooperation from February 23rd 2010 to October 3rd 2011.
Minister of the Interior and Social Affairs from April 7th 2009 to February 23rd 2010.

Spokeswoman on social affairs 2014-2015, primary school and youth education 2011-2014, family policy, immigration and integration 2007-2009. Member of the Children's and Education Committee, the Social Affairs Committee, the Gender Equality Committee, the Immigration and Integration  Affairs Committe and the delegation to the Nordic Council 2011-2015. Member of the Social Services Committee, the Foreign Affairs Committee and the Immigration and Integration Affairs Committee 2007-2009.


Education and jobs
Trained as a school teacher, N. Zahles College of Education, from 2002 to 2004.
Matriculated, Holte Upper Secondary School, from 1986 to 1989.

Teacher, Rungsted School, from 2003 to 2007.
Self-employed journalist, from 1999 to 2002.
Day-to-day manager, Dagmar Theatre, from 1996 to 1999.
Head of Administration, Brinkmann Kommunikation, from 1995 to 1996.
Day-to-day manager, Scandinavian Models, from 1993 to 1995.
Consultant, The Voice, from 1990 to 1993.


Affiliations
Former chairwoman of the private foundation Elleslettegård. Former member of the management committee of 'Fonden for Social Ansvar' (The Fund for Social Responsibility) (including 'Natteravnene'). Former member of the management committee of 'Foreningen Østifterne'. Former chairwoman of The Norden Association. Former member of the board of 'Fonden Frie Børnehaver'.
