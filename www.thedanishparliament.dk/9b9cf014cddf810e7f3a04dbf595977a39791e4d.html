Karin Gaardsted, born October 19th 1955 in Ørre Parish, Herning, daughter of farmer Børge Gaardsted Rasmussen and Anna Rasmussen. Mother of Nana Cecilie Gaardsted Bøvling, born in 1991.


Member period
Member of Parliament for the Social Democratic Party in Western Jutland greater constituency from September 15th 2011.
Candidate for the Social Democratic Party in Viborg West nomination district from 2010.

Parliamentary career

Spokeswoman on IT and telecommunications, consumer policy, Greenland and the Faroe Islands. Vice-chairwoman of the Energy, Utilities and Climate Committee from 2015.


Education and jobs
Trained as a schoolteacher at Herning Teacher Training College, from 1975 to 1979.

R&D Manager with the Municipal Association of School Libraries in Denmark, from 2002 to 2009.
Project Consultant, Children's Culture Council, Ministry of Culture, from 2000 to 2002.
Children's Culture Consultant, Viborg Municipality, from 1990 to 2000.
Primary school teacher, Houlkær School, Viborg, 1990.


Affiliations
Member of the National Council for Children 1995-1998. Member of Viborg Town Council 2006-2011, Deputy Mayor, Chairwoman of the Children and Youth Committee, Vice-chairwoman of the Economic Affairs Committee, Member of the Business and Development Committee and of the Democracy Committee. Member of the Local Tax Appeals Tribunal, Skive-Viborg 2010-2011. Chairwoman of the Property Foundation at Agro Business Park 2010-2011. Member of the Board of Directors of Agro Business Park 2010-2011. Chairwoman of 'Kulturprinsen', the national children's culture centre 2010-2011. Member of the management board of 'STU', specially-arranged youth education programmes, Viborg 2010-2011. Member of 'Praktikforum', Central Jutland Region 2010-2011. Member of Baltic Sea Parliamentary Conference from 2015. Member of the control committee of the Nordic Investment Bank from 2015.


Publications
Author of »Skolekunst - en håndbog om kultur« (School Art – a cultural manual), Kommunernes Skolebiblioteksforening, 2008.
